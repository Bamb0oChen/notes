    翻译押题：

    中国已建成全球规模领先的可再生能源发电体系之一。

    China has established a renewable energy power generation system that among the top of the world.

    • that among the top of the world.<->(world's leading)
    • 规模.<->(in terms of scale)

    - 注意词伙（词块）例如：可再生能源发电体系 renewable energy power generation sysytems

    近年来，风能和太阳能发展迅速，清洁能源在能源结构中的比重不断提升。

    Recent years, wind energy and solar energy has been being sharply developed, the amount of the clean energy in the energy structure is growing steadily

    • developed (形态上发生了变化) <-> expand (业务扩大，数量更多) expand:扩充，扩展自己的想法 developed:to (cause something to) grow or change into a more advanced, larger, or stronger form（使）发展；（使）发育；（使）成长
    • 不确定是不是因果？ -> 伴随with
    • 占据……比重 -> accounting for
    • 份额amount -> share(a part of something that has been divided between several people)

    - In recent years,rapid expansion in wind and solar power has driven(表示因果) a steady rise in theshare of clean energy within the overall energy mix(用来展示‘清洁能源’和所有能源的区别)

    目前，中国的可再生能源装机(China's installed renewable energy)规模在全球处于领先地位。

    So far, China has a world-leading scale of fixed renewable energy generator.

    • so far <-> at present
    • 规模 -> (scale/ capacity) scale:大范围，模糊的大，不点明方向 capacity:数量上大
    • a world-leading <-> ranks among the leading levels

    可再生能源的发展不仅有效缓解了能源供应压力，也显著减少了碳排放，改善了生态(ecological)环境。

    The development of renewable energy not only alleviates the pressure of energy supply, but significantly decline the carbon emission, Improve the environment as well.

    • the pressure of energy supply <-> energy-supply pressure
    • The development of renewable energy has not only helped ease pressure on energysupply,but also led to significant reductions(名词化) in carbon emissions and improvements in theecological environment

    同时，中国在新能源技术研发、设备制造和工程建设方面积累的经验，正在为全球能源转型提供重要支持。

    Meanwhile, the experience, accumulated as China proceeds in the aspects like new energy technology researching, equipment manufacturing and construction engineering(n. 工程学), are greatly supporting the global energy transformation.

    • construction engineering -> engineering construction
    • 可以用同位语做转折：a source ofimportant support for the global energy transition.

    这一进展表明，中国正以实际行动推动绿色发展，并为应对全球气候变化作出积极贡献。

    This progress indicates that, China is promoting the green development with valid action, and making positive contribution to the global climate change.

    • vaild -> concrete
    • make contribution 背后要跟解决问题

    小巧思&分割线：达者兼济天下，穷者独善其身：Empowered, care outward; constrained, keep integrity
