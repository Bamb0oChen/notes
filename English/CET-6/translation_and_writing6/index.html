    写作两篇（偶像的作用必背）

    这篇文章和之前的思路有点不一样，这篇文章主要还是模仿高中文章的构思，好(有可取之处)--坏(如果过度会怎么样)--辩证看待(推至极端，分类讨论)

    For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay that begins with thesentence "There is a growing trend among young people to look up to actors andsingers as their role models. "You can make comments, cite examples or use yourpersonal experience to develop your essay.You should write at least 150 words butno more than 200 words.

    写法一

    资本——全面的偶像——外貌——成绩也很好（资本的包装和渲染）

    There is a growing trend among young people to look up to actors and singers as their role models.

    While …… 能走到行业顶端的人一定有值得认可的品质 ……（榜样的作用），但是（转折）娱乐资本把明星塑造成“完美人设”，会产生 …… 的后果

    Those who rise to the top of entertainment industry often possess admirable qualitities --- talent, discipline and perseverance shaped by years of intense competition.

    The real concern arises when the entertainment industry begins to present public figures as nearly flawless individuals. What reaches (造成的另一种表达) audience is 有血有肉的人（用否定增强气势）flesh-and-blood person with contradictions and imperfections but a carfully crafted public persona. Leading many to assume that such polished lives are normal and attainable.

    Imperfection is an unavoidable condition of human life.It is therefore absurd that themodern entertainment industry often behaves as if the basic truth could be erased.

    Traditionally respected role models-such as scientists, educators, orthinkers-have never been admired because they lived flawless lives. They, too,were individuals with limitations, yet their achievements, ideas, and long-termdedication to society offered inspiration to others. More importantly, even amid thelimitations, setbacks, and imperfections of human nature,they were still able topersist in the pursuit of meaningful work. Contemporary entertainment idols, by contrast, are often elevated(这个好) into symbols of total wxcellence. From appearance to talent, even personality and lifestyle, they are portrayed as if success permeated(弥漫，散布) every dimension of their lives. Even when such excellence is uneven or exaggerated, publicity systems quickly step in their image, while devoted fans(狂热粉) readily explain away the inconsistencies.

    Once the (complexities of real life emerge)好强的表达 , the illusion of perfection rarely survives.Admiration, properly understood, is not anembrace of perfection, but a recognition that the essence of human life lies instriving within the boundaries of imperfection.

    好，最后一段总结文章 The challenge is not to reject idls but to approach them with discernment. One may admire a celebrity's talent---even a Roman-statue-like face, such as Mel Gibson's in his prime---yet few would treat such figures as templates for a life partner,particularly when their private lives have been marked by violence or controversy.
