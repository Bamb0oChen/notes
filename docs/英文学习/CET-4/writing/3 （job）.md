**简单版：**

**As requirements for job applications are getting increasingly higher, college students ought to be better prepared for their future career. This advice may sound ordinary, even ==tiresome==, because it has been repeated countless times. Yet the pattern rarely changes: many students dismiss it until the final hour, when calm planning gives way to frantic cramming. In today’s unpredictable world, such neglect is no longer a minor flaw but a costly gamble.**

**随着求职要求不断提高，大学生理应为未来的职业做好更充分的准备。这条建议听起来或许普通，甚至有些令人厌倦，因为它已经被无数次重复。然而，情况几乎从未改变：许多学生总是拖到最后一刻才重视准备，原本应当从容的规划，往往演变成慌乱的临时抱佛脚。在当今充满不确定性的世界里，这种忽视已不再是小小的缺陷，而是一场代价高昂的赌博。**

**Genuine preparation today goes far beyond earning a diploma. In the past, a degree could almost guarantee security, but now it merely opens the first door. Globalization, automation and artificial intelligence are reshaping industries at breathtaking speed, and employers demand more than textbook knowledge. They seek digital competence, creative thinking, emotional resilience, and above all, the humility to keep learning. To be prepared, therefore, is not to pass a single exam, but to adopt readiness as a lifelong discipline. Without this mindset, graduates may not be defeated by competitors, but by the very pace of change.**

**如今的真正准备，早已远远超出了获得一纸文凭的范围。过去，一份学位几乎能够保证安全感，但现在，它仅仅意味着敲开第一扇门。全球化、自动化和人工智能正以惊人的速度重塑产业，雇主们需要的不只是课本上的知识。他们看重的是数字能力、创造性思维、情绪的韧性，以及最重要的，持续学习的谦逊心态。因此，所谓准备，并不是通过一场考试，而是把“随时待命”视作一生的修炼。缺乏这样的心态，毕业生的落败并非输给竞争者，而是被时代的脚步所淘汰。**

**Making adequate preparation**  **is no longer optional; it has become essential. Yet some still sneer at this idea because luck once carried them through an exam. To them I can only say: you’d better pray fortune never deserts you, or your so** **called strategy will collapse faster than a summer study plan.**

**准备不再是可选项，而是必需品。然而，仍有人对此嗤之以鼻，因为他们曾经靠运气通过过一场考试。对此，我只能说：你最好祈祷好运永远不会离开你，否则你那所谓的“策略”，崩塌得比暑假学习计划还要快。**

**正式版：**

**As requirements for job applications are getting increasingly higher, college students ought to be better prepared for their future career. While this advice may appear predictable, its relevance has only intensified. The same scenario repeats itself year after year: reminders are ignored, plans are postponed, and genuine preparation begins only when deadlines are unavoidable. In an era defined by rapid transformation, such behavior is not a trivial oversight but a fundamental liability.**

**随着求职要求不断提高，大学生理应为未来的职业做好更充分的准备。 虽然这条建议看似老生常谈，但其重要性却愈发凸显。年复一年，同样的场景不断重演：提醒被忽视，计划被推迟，真正的准备总是在截止日期迫在眉睫时才开始。在一个以迅速变革为特征的时代，这种行为不再是小小的疏忽，而是一种根本性的负担。**

**True readiness today extends far beyond the possession of a diploma. Once, academic credentials could secure stable employment. Now they function merely as an entry point, insufficient in themselves. Globalization, automation, and artificial intelligence are reshaping industries with unprecedented speed, dismantling old certainties and creating new demands. Employers increasingly prize competencies that cannot be memorized in a week—digital fluency, analytical thinking, emotional resilience, and, above all, the capacity for continuous learning. Preparation, therefore, must be understood not as a shortlived rite of passage but as a sustained commitment to adaptability and growth. Without this perspective, graduates are not outperformed by more diligent peers; rather, they are overtaken by the structural shifts of the labor market itself.**

**如今的真正准备，早已远远超出了拥有一纸文凭的范畴。过去，学历可以带来稳定的就业；但如今，它仅仅是一个入场券，本身已不足以支撑职业发展。全球化、自动化和人工智能正以前所未有的速度重塑产业，打破旧有的确定性，并提出新的要求。雇主越来越重视那些无法在一周之内死记硬背的能力——数字素养、分析性思维、情绪的韧性，以及最重要的，持续学习的能力。因此，准备必须被理解为对适应性与成长的长期承诺，而不是一段短暂的过渡。缺乏这种视角，毕业生并非败给更勤奋的同龄人，而是被劳动力市场的结构性变革所淘汰。**

**The conclusion is unmistakable: Making adequate preparation**  **has ceased to be optional and has become indispensable. Yet some students persist in relying on luck, mistaking occasional success for sustainable achievement. Such reliance yields nothing more than ephemeral gains, and in today’s world, it is a gamble very few can afford to make.**

**结论显而易见：准备已不再是可有可无的，而是不可或缺的。然而，一些学生仍执迷于依赖运气，把偶然的成功误认为是可以长期维持的成就。这种依赖所带来的不过是短暂的收获，而在当今世界，这是一个几乎无人能够承担的赌博。**
