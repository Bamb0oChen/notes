"You can make comments, cite examples or use your personalexperience to develop your essay. You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words

## 批注（仿照前几篇的词块标注法）

### 段一（开头 + 立意拔高）
`exert a profound influence on`：CET-6 影响题万能动词搭配，后面接 `sb's ...` 或 `the way ...`。

`academic pursuit and personal development`：并列结构很稳；`pursuit` 比 `study` 更正式。

`When ..., and ..., teachers protect ...: ...`：用 `When A, and B, C do D: E` 这种“背景压力 → 主体行动 → 冒号拔高”结构，开头气势很足。

`answers are offered by AI before questions are even fully formed`：
	- 亮点：`before ... are fully formed` 很书面，表达“问题都没想清楚就先给答案”。
	- 可替换：`before students have even articulated the question`（更具体）。

`success is reduced to mere numbers on a screen`：
	- `be reduced to` = 被简化为/沦为；`mere` = “仅仅是”，强烈贬义色彩。
	- 可替换：`success is measured only by`（更直白更不冒险）。

`something that is quietly fading away`：`quietly` 让语气更高级，表达“悄然流失”。

`the essence of independent thought`：`the essence of ...` 是典型议论文升格词块。

`the cultivation of moral character`：`cultivation of` + 抽象名词（character/virtue/responsibility）很学术。

`(multi)dimensional standards of evaluation`：
	- 写作里 `evaluation` 比 `judge` 更正式。
	- 更常见写法：`multidimensional criteria for evaluation`。

`critically engage with the world`：
	- `engage with` 比 `face`/`deal with` 更有“互动、参与”的感觉。
	- 可替换：`think critically about the world`（更保险）。

`rather than passively consume it`：对照很清晰：`critically engage` vs `passively consume`。

### 段二（论证：机制 + 教师的作用）
`the defenders of the "human" aspect of education`：
	- `defender` 有“守护者/捍卫者”气质，适合这篇主题。
	- 可替换：`guardians of ...`（语气更柔和）。

`knowledge arrives filtered and compressed by recommendation systems`：
	- `arrives + 过去分词` 是很地道的书面写法。
	- 可替换：`information is filtered and packaged by algorithms`（更常见）。

`access conclusions within seconds`：
	- `access` 作动词很正式；也可用 `reach`/`get`。
	- 搭配：`reach a conclusion`（注意 `conclusion` 常与 `reach/draw` 搭）。

`without tracing the reasoning behind them`：
	- `trace` = 追溯（推理链）；非常适合写“跳过过程”。
	- 可替换：`without working through the logic`。

`the abundance of information has surged`：`abundance` + `surge` 强对比（多了，但…）。

`sustained attention`：固定词块 = 持续注意力。

`independent judgment increasingly deteriorate`：
	- 这里更建议语法上用复数一致：`judgment ... deteriorates`。
	- 可替换：`independent judgment is gradually weakened`（更稳）。

`be tasked with doing`：万能被动结构：`be tasked with + V-ing`。

`fostering analytical thinking rather than merely providing answers`：
	- `foster` = 培养；比 `develop` 更“教育语境”。
	- `rather than merely ...` 很适合写“不是…而是…”。

`immerse themselves in entire works instead of resorting to summaries`：
	- `immerse oneself in`（沉浸于）是高分词块。
	- `resort to`（诉诸/不得不依赖）带一点“无奈/偷懒”评价色彩。

`resist the idea that ...`：议论文常用“反对某种观念”的引导。

`be measured entirely by metrics`：
	- `entirely` 加强语气；`metrics` = 指标（比 `numbers` 更正式）。
	- 可替换：`be reduced to test scores and rankings`（更贴近校园）。

`a culture dominated by rankings, scores, and visibility`：
	- `dominated by` 是“被…主导”的模板表达。
	- `visibility` 适合写“被看见/曝光度”的评价体系。

`define themselves by comparison`：很典型的价值观批判句式。

`illuminate mistakes, recognize effort and reframe failure`：
	- 三连动词并列很加分（节奏感）。
	- `reframe` = 重新框定/重新理解（很学术）。

`an essential part of personal growth`：万能收束短语。

### 段三（总结 + 金句）
`not merely ... that ...`：CET-6 常用的“不是…而是…”变体（更书面）。

`dispense facts`：
	- `dispense` = 分发/提供（正式）；也可用 `deliver`。
	- 可替换：`spit out facts`（更口语，不建议考试用）。

`increasingly enamored with`：
	- `enamored with` = 痴迷于（偏文学）；更稳的是 `obsessed with`。

`instant gratification`：固定高分词块 = 即时满足。

`unsung superheroes`：比喻很强、略偏“口语热血”；更稳妥替代：`quiet guardians` / `often-overlooked protectors`。

`preserving our capacity for "slow thinking"`：
	- `capacity for` 是抽象名词搭配。
	- `slow thinking` 可替换为更学术的 `deliberate thinking`。

`even though we can breeze through a meme ...`：
	- 反差句很好，但考试里尽量避免太网络化的 `meme`；可替换：`short clips` / `snackable content`。

### 快速可替换句式（考场备用）
`In this context, teachers are tasked with ...` → `Against this backdrop, teachers are expected to ...`

`value can be measured entirely by metrics` → `value should not be defined solely by test scores and rankings`

`preserve our capacity for slow thinking` → `keep alive our ability to think patiently and independently`
